# Walkthrough: 'Aetheria: Chrono-Slide Escape'

A premium sliding-based escape room game. Every lock, conduit, and system relay is resolved by sliding grids (3x3 or 4x4) to form patterns, alignments, or connections.

---

## 🚪 Chamber 1: The Induction Vault
1. **Floor Grate**: Click the grate at the bottom center to pick up the `Thermal Screwdriver` and `Stabilizer Shard`.
2. **Hex Screws**: Select the `Thermal Screwdriver` from your Chronopack and click the **Wall Terminal Lock** cover plate to unscrew it.
3. **Glyph Slider (Number Slider)**: Click the terminal console to launch the 3x3 Number Slider. Slide tiles to arrange numbers 1-8 in order. Solving it drops a `Thermal Battery`.
4. **Relay Core**: Click the `Thermal Battery` in your pack, then click the **Power Relay Cell** socket.
5. **Circuit Link (Circuit Slider)**: Click the **Exit Door** terminal to launch the 3x3 Circuit Slider. Slide the wire conduit tiles to form a continuous glowing path from the Left Input node to the Right Output node. Step through the Door to enter Chamber 2.

## 🔮 Chamber 2: The Chrono-Core Room
1. **Tool Rack**: Click the rack to collect a `Depleted Battery` and `Aether Core Charge`.
2. **Combine Battery**: Select the `Depleted Battery` and combine it with the `Aether Core Charge` to craft a `Charged Battery`.
3. **Backup Power**: Slot the `Charged Battery` into the **Backup Generator** on the left to restore stability levels.
4. **Crystal Tuner (Cipher Slider)**: Click the **Tuner Lock Console** on the right to launch the 3x3 Cipher Slider. Slide the fragmented holographic segments to reconstruct the golden key drawing. The drawer opens; collect the `Optic Prism` and `Stabilizer Shard`.
5. **Optic Laser (Laser Slider)**: Slot the `Optic Prism` into the **Laser Deflector Base** projector. Click it to launch the 3x3 Laser Slider. Slide mirror tiles (`/` and `\`) to bounce the laser beam from the top-left Emitter to the bottom-right Receiver.
6. **Survival Choice**: Click the active Gateway terminal. Choose to brace focus (-20 Focus) or vent reactor coolant (-15 Stability) to proceed.

## 🌌 Chamber 3: The Aether Portal
1. **Console Desk**: Click the desk on the right to grab the `Optic Lens`, `Tension Clip`, and `Neodymium Magnet`.
2. **Lockpick**: Combine `Tension Clip` and `Neodymium Magnet` to craft a `Magnetic Lockpick`.
3. **Decipher Wall Screen**: Select `Optic Lens` and click the **Magnifier Pedestal** on the left to reveal the code: `8-2-0`.
4. **Trunk**: Select the `Magnetic Lockpick` and click the **Locked Chest** on the left. Collect the golden `Aether Portal Key`.
5. **Laser Generator (Laser Slider)**: Click the **Laser Core Generator** on the right. Launch the 3x3 Laser Slider and route the beam into the receiver by sliding mirrors.
6. **Activate Control**: Slot the `Aether Portal Key` into the **Portal Control Console** under the vortex.
7. **Relay Node (Circuit Slider)**: Click the console again to launch the 3x3 Circuit Slider. Slide tiles to connect the left and right terminals.
8. **Next Zone**: Click the glowing portal archway to proceed to Chamber 4.

## 🌀 Chamber 4: The Quantum Resonance Gate
1. **Floor Card**: Click the ground card on the left to pick up the `Access Key Card`.
2. **Quantum Core**: Click the glowing pod on the right to pick up a `Resonance Shard` (stabilizer).
3. **Resonance Terminal (Circuit Slider)**: Click the **Resonance Grid** terminal on the left to launch the 4x4 Circuit Slider. Slide conduit tiles to form a link from the Left to Right terminal.
4. **Validation**: Select the `Access Key Card` and swipe it on the **Portal Console**.
5. **Exit**: Click the door to enter Chamber 5.

## ⏳ Chamber 5: The Chronos Gateway (Final Stage)
1. **Equipment Shelf**: Click the rack on the bottom left to grab `Empty Aether Cell` and `Aether Plasma Fluid`.
2. **Combine Cell**: Combine `Empty Aether Cell` and `Aether Plasma Fluid` to create a `Charged Aether Cell`.
3. **Insert Cell**: Slot the `Charged Aether Cell` into the **Gate Power Cell Cage** on the left.
4. **Mirror Refractor (Laser Slider)**: Click the **Laser Grid Panel** on the right to launch the 4x4 Laser Slider. Slide mirrors to route the beam to the receiver.
5. **Crystal Lock (Cipher Slider)**: Click the **Engage Portal Control Console** at the bottom center to launch the 4x4 Cipher Slider. Reconstruct the complex golden chronokey schematic.
6. **Temporal Escape**: Click the swirling active blue **Gate Vortex** to escape the loop and win the game!
